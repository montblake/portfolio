Trivia game featuring data from third party APIs with a database implementation designed to reduce API calls over time.

The parlor game Six Degrees of Kevin Bacon was inspired by the concept that any two people in the world could be linked by six personal connections or fewer. The game begins with some actor from a Hollywood film. The challenge is to link them — through films they have worked on and actors with whom they have worked — to Kevin Bacon with six degrees of separation or less.

Unfortunately, the current state of the app has a big drawback. It is SLOW! If the info is in the database, everything works great. If not, the current version has to collect ALL of the information before it returns ANYTHING. All fifty movie casts must be individually looked up before you can even see the actor's picture.
